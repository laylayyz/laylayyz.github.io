CLEAN SPACE, CLEAN EARTH

Excess space junk surrounding earth causes more pollution to earth's atmosphere, resulting in more danger for the future of space exploration.

We want more reusable and eco-friendly spacecraft being built to leave minimal impact on the solar system and our atmosphere.


Space X's reusable Falcon 9 Rocket is a great example of an eco-friendly rocket.

It is considered a great rocket because it's first stage booster can land back on earth and can be used again, unlike other disposable rockets.
