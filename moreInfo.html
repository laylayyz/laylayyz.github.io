So, what is Space Junk?


Space junk, or orbital debris, refers to the non-functional and inactive man-made objects in earth's orbit.


Some types of space junk are:


Defunct Satellites

Rocket Bodies

Debris Fragments

Why is it a problem?


High speed collisions risk damaging satellites and the ISS (International Space Station).

More debris = harder to access space safely and further expand space exploration.
